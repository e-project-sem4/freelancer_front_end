General Questions :

What is Topwork.com?

Topwork.com is an effective and safe freelance job website in Vietnam. We provide an online working tool that allows both parties to work and exchange directly, minimizing unnecessary disputes and risks.

How to post a job and find a freelancer like that?

After successfully creating an account and activating it, you just need to click on “Create job”, choose the type of job, the name of the job you need to perform. Continue to enter the amount of money you can pay for the job in the “Payment Amount” field. You can add skill requirements for freelancers at “Add skills”. Finish the job posting by clicking “Post a job”. After your work is posted, you can choose the right freelancer based on the freelancers' offers, profiles and customer reviews. Alternatively you can choose to find a freelancer in our list of available freelancers by clicking “Hire freelancer” and using the “Search” mode.

What is the difference between Topwork.com and traditional job websites in Vietnam today?

Topwork.com is a freelance job website, which means that as long as you have passion, you can completely get a job and do it besides your main job. We focus on projects that can be completed within a certain period of time and are not long term jobs, so anyone can participate in the activities on our website. Besides, vLance.vn also created an online website for users to work and resolve all disputes right on the system.
